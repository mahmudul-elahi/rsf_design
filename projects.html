R.S.F. Israel (EV) Ltd. operates in the field of audio guidance and multimedia, and specializes in providing technological solutions and content services to enrich the visiting experience at museums, tourist sites and heritage sites. The company provides a wide range of fixed and mobile multimedia systems, including: video and audio players, audio guidance and multimedia systems for individual visitors or groups, in a variety of languages ​​and for different sectors.
